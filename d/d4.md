## Horadric Cube

### Gear mod

#### Add Affix

Adds a random Affix to an item. Use a *Tuning Prism* to narrow the Affix Category.
Required Materials
1 Common, Magic, Rare or Legendary Item.
1 Coarse Primordial Dust
5 Raw Primordial Dust
1 Tuning Prism (optional) (narrow the affix category)

#### Chaotic Reroll

Changes a random Affix to one of another Category.
Use a Tuning Prism to determine the Category of the random chosen Affix.
Required Materials
1 Magic, Rare or Legendary item
1 Refined Primordial Dust
15 Raw Primordial Dust
1 Tuning Prism (optional) (narrow the category)

#### Focused Reroll

Changes an Affix to one of the same Category. 
Requires a Tuning Prism to determine the Category.
Required Materials
1 Magic, Rare or Legendary Item
1 Refined Primordial Dust
15 Raw Primordial Dust
1 Tuning Prism

#### Remove Affix

Removes a random Affix from an Item.
Use a Tuning Prism to narrow the random chosen Affix.
Required Materials
1 Magic or Rare item
1 Refined Primordial Dust
15 Raw Primordial Dust
1 Tuning Prism (Optional)

#### Transfigure Item

Grants a random modification of great possibilities!
Use a Tuning Prism to narrow the outcomes.
The item has a ==very high chance== to become ==unmodifiable== after Transmogrification.
Required Materials
1 Legendary, Unique or Mythic item
1 Volatile Primordial Dust
1 Tuning Prism (Optional)

#### Unique Power Reroll

Randomizes the value of the Unique Power of an Ancestral Unique Item
Required Materials
1 Ancestral Unique
1 Attuned Primordial Dust
100 Raw Primordial Dust
